    each card has an ease factor (starts at 2.5) and an interval in days. when you grade a card, quality is mapped: again=0, hard=3, good=4, easy=5.

    new ease becomes old + (0.1 - (5 - q) * (0.08 + (5 - q) * 0.02)), clamped to a minimum of 1.3.

    if quality is below 3, the card resets: interval back to 1 day, repetition count to 0. otherwise the first review schedules 1 day out, the second 6 days out, and after that the new interval is the previous one multiplied by the ease.

    next review is just now + interval days.
